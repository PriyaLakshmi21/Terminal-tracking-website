Help & Support

How can I find my terminal?

You can use the Search page to select your airline and check which terminal and gate you need to go to.

Where can I find a terminal map?

Visit the Map page to view the full terminal layout along with important sections like check-in, security, and baggage claim.

How do I check for flight delays or alerts?

Go to the Alerts page to view live updates about delays, weather disruptions, and terminal changes.

What should I do if I need assistance?

Please visit the airport information desk or contact our support team. Details are available at the airport help centers.
